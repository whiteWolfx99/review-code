Air Fryer Keto Chicken Wings

These keto air fryer chicken wings are finished in under 15 minutes and are a great dish if you are on the keto diet. If desired, separate wings into drumettes.

Prep: 5 mins

Cook: 15 mins

Total: 25 mins

Ingredients serving 5

  • 3 pounds chicken wings

  • 1 tablespoon taco seasoning mix

  • 2 teaspoons olive oil

Directions

  • step 1 Combine chicken wings, taco seasoning, and oil in a resealable plastic bag. Shake to coat.

  • step 2 Preheat the air fryer to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C) for 2 minutes.

  • step 3 Place wings in the air fryer and cook for 12 minutes, flipping after 6 minutes.
